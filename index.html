Orcd

orcD, or Operational Rated CDR Dashboard is a proof of concept to visualize rated CDR’s on a heat and world map. It includes a HeatMap for visualizing CDR’s that has gone through some rating/billing system which has a product tied to it. The GeoMap visualizes rated CDR’s which has a roaming status in the record.

The project was made at the request of Qvantel and was later made open source.

For this project I was the CI-master and a developer in the backend team.
